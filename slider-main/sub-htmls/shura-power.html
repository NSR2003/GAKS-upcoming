Shura Power:

Powering Tomorrow, Today: Shura Power Clean, Convenient EV Charging Vision

Creating sustainable transportation is no longer a luxury, and Shura Power is happy to be at the forefront, both literally and figuratively.

Being an ambitious clean energy startup, we are re-inventing the concept of electric vehicle (EV) charging, integrating with solar power, Vertical Axis Wind Turbines (VAWTs), and other customer experiences to design an exclusive green charging eco-system that extends way beyond the plug.

Clean Energy. Clean Commute.

Our mission statement is based on clean, renewable energy. The transportation and energy industries are very dominated by fossil fuel but their effects on the environment and their exhaustibility has compelled a change. Shura Power provides environmentally conscious EV owners with an even more responsible option: the charging lounges that are solar and wind powered, not just any wind turbines.

We use Vertical Axis Wind Turbines (VAWTs), which are small, very efficient, and suitable for the urban and semi-urban setting. As opposed to the conventional horizontal-axis turbines, VAWTs work effectively even under the conditions of variable and turbulent winds, which is why they are ideal to be placed both roadside and commercially. These turbines are silent, take up little area, and complement solar panels to create a hybrid renewable system that guarantees a supply of energy even during the sunny and dark hours.

Solar-Wind Hybrid: The Best Out of Nature

Our charging stations will have a hybrid system that will combine photovoltaic solar panels and VAWTs. This mix makes sure that the generation of power is not limited to the hours of daylight or changing wind patterns that occur at various seasons. Our system is designed to distribute energy reliably and sustainably, regardless of the hot, afternoon sun or a breeze of the evening drive.

The difference is our advantageous strategy of capturing energy that Shura Power has made a focus of attention: solar energy will be used when there is maximum sunlight; VAWTs will be used when it is cloudy or at night. This two-pronged strategy will cut tremendously on reliance on grid electricity, operational costs, and most importantly, reduce carbon emissions.

Side of Comfort - Charging with the Shura Experience

Although our focus on renewable energy is in our core, what makes Shura Power a unique model is our customer-centric charging experience. We are convinced that it should not be a boring and time-consuming task to recharge your EV; it should be a pleasant stop on the way.

Every Shura Power station shall be combined with a quick-service restaurant or cafe, where drivers and passengers can get a healthy snack, meal, or drink, ensuring that their car is charging. No matter where you might be going, whether it is a long-distance trip on the interstate or simply a local commute, our charging stations provide you with an opportunity to recharge, take a break, and get some energy back in your battery and yourself.

This special Charge & Chill experience is altering the interaction of people with EV infrastructure. Plugging in is one thing, but it is all about making a community space that runs on sustainability and service. Our ecosystem is user-friendly with clean bathrooms, Wi-Fi, and food and beverages that use locally sourced ingredients.

Efficiency and Sustainability: Our Technology Backbone

All Shura Power plants are built on the ground with intelligent load management and complex energy controllers. These systems keep an eye on the production, consumption, and battery status of energy to perform optimally. It could be a matter of balancing energy supply between solar and wind and storage, or prioritising fast charging based on availability, but either way, our technology will keep you up and running and as efficient as possible.

We know that EV drivers want their charge to be quick and reliable, and that is what we provide with multi-gun charging installations, each of which can supply multiple EVs at once. We also have infrastructure that is designed to handle 150kW DC fast chargers and can be scaled in a modular manner based on traffic and usage trends of the stations.

The Strategic Edge: Power Unseen

Although we are still focused on the end-user experience and renewable generation, Shura Power also incorporates the most progressive Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS) to guarantee constant charging and smart energy storage.

These systems silently operate in the background, saving surplus renewable energy when it's being generated in abundance and releasing it when the maximum demand is being experienced or when clouds block the wind. This internal architecture helps to increase the station's reliability, minimize the grid draw, and adhere to our green operations. We are not going to go into all the details, but our BESS strategy is one of the key distinguishing factors-- it allows us to keep our promise, even when nature fails.
