FREDUCE:

Fresh, Real, Clean Food Delivered in less than 24 Hours of Harvest

In a world where everything is grown with chemicals, where food supply chains are long and food is nutritionally deficient, FREDUCE is restoring what never should have been lost: pure, fresh, and responsibly grown vegetables- delivered within 24 hours of harvest.

FREDUCE, an abbreviation of Fresh Produce, is the new daring solution to a healthy future in India. Our uncompromising attitude to quality, safety, and nutrition is redefining how people get their food, beginning with how it is grown, to how fast it gets to your plate.

A New Freshness Standard

At FREDUCE, fresh is not a marketing slogan; it is a promise. Our produce is picked and shipped in less than 24 hours, which means minimal nutrient loss, maximum shelf life, and optimum taste. FREDUCE reduces the time it takes to get the product to the shelves by cutting down the farm-to-fork process to less than a day, unlike the traditional supply chains, which take days or even weeks. This fast turnaround time saves:

  • Nutritional value
  • Natural flavor and aroma
  • Aesthetics and sharpness

When you eat with FREDUCE, you are eating produce that was growing yesterday.

The Reason We Said No to Soil

Conventional farming has become rampant with heavy metal contamination, pesticide residues, and unsafe irrigation practices. These invisible poisons build up unnoticed in the food chain and lead to chronic health problems such as digestive disorders, hormone imbalance, and even cancer.

Our answer? Hydroponics Controlled Environment Agriculture.

FREDUCE crops are all grown in:

  • Purified RO water
  • Devoid of soil (which removes the risk of exposure to heavy metal residues)
  • In completely observed indoor conditions where nutrients are controlled automatically

Not only does this render our produce clean and safe, it also renders it consistent in quality, year-round. No seasons, no weather dependencies, no surprises.

What We Grow: Not Just Instagram Greens, but Everyday Essentials

The majority of hydroponic startups pursue exotic greens: lettuce, kale, arugula, and broccoli. Although they have a niche, they seldom reach the Indian kitchen every day. FREDUCE is not that way. We cultivate what India consumes.

We are interested in:

  • Sorrel Leaves (Gongura) - a South Indian household staple
  • Cherry tomatoes- they go well with salads and Indian curries
  • Amaranthus (Thotakura) - very nutritious and locally popular
  • Capsicum (Bell Peppers) - colorful, crispy, and rich in Vitamin C
  • Other leafy vegetables, which are the mainstay of Indian cooking
  • Spinach (Palak) - iron-rich and can be used in dal, parathas, and curries

This will make us relevant, affordable, and in tandem with the daily Indian food habits, besides enhancing the nutritional density of the meals of the average family.
